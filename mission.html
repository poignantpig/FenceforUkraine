Our Mission

Our Goal

To reach out and help fencers affected by the war in Ukraine

Our Big Question

We noticed that tons of beginner fencers were struggling with simple tactics and ideas. We wanted to find a way to help them as well as helping something near and dear to my heart right now. The war in Ukraine has affected everyone right now. Tons of displaced people and fencers as well as clubs out of business. Could we find a way to help everyone?

Our Initiative

An intermediate/beginner level kid will fence with an instructor. As they are fencing the instructor gives tactical advice as well as general tips. Ex: “Make sure to keep your legs bent” “Step back on the parry” “Notice how I disengaged around your blade here.” All the money paid through these private lessons will go directly to Ukranian fencers affected by the war.

About Us

We are a group of high school students who wish to support those struggling in Ukraine due to the Russian-Ukraine war
