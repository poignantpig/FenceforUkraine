Our Goal

Fence for Ukraine is a project created and led by high school students aiming to help fencers and clubs who have been affected by the cruel war in Ukraine. This is done by offering sparring with the competitive students. Instead of sending money to vague charities Fencing for Ukraine aims to help specific clubs recover and get back to their old routine.

Our Mission

More Info

The student will fence with the competitive fencer and receive advice and tactics through 20-minute sparring for 20$. The premise is to improve the younger fencer, as fencing with someone very experienced is the best way to improve. On the spreadsheet, you can choose the time and with whom you would like to spar with. The schedule is on a weekly basis and will be updated monthly. The money will be donated to struggling clubs in Ukraine.
